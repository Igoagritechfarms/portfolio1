About Walkathon Event

The IGO Group of Companies proudly organized a refreshing Walkathon on Sunday, 14th morning from 6:00 AM to 8:00 AM at Besant Nagar Beach. The event brought together fitness enthusiasts, families, and community members for a morning filled with energy, wellness, and positivity. To make the walk even more impactful, special fitness and stretching sessions were conducted by expert coaches Mr. Mowli and Mr. Joshwa, guiding participants on the importance of proper warm-up, stretching, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. With the sound of the waves, the morning breeze, and the spirit of togetherness, the Walkathon created a perfect atmosphere to inspire fitness and unity. It was not just a walk but a celebration of health, happiness, and community bonding.
